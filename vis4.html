Do you see the Grey dots at the points of intersection ?

Hermann grid illusion


There are not gray dots in this grid. However “ghostlike” gray blobs are perceived at the intersections of the white lines.
The gray dots disappear when looking directly at an intersection.
This illusion can be explained by a neural process happening in the visual system called
Lateral inhibition
(the capacity of an active neuron to reduce the activity of its neighbors).
